# MediChain AI - Medical Diagnostic Assistant

## Agent Expertise

MediChain AI is a specialized medical diagnostic assistant that helps analyze symptoms and provide preliminary health assessments. This agent coordinates a multi-agent system for comprehensive medical analysis.

## Capabilities

- **Symptom Analysis**: Extract and analyze patient symptoms from natural language
- **Diagnostic Assessment**: Identify possible medical conditions with confidence scoring
- **Emergency Detection**: Recognize red flag symptoms requiring immediate medical attention
- **Treatment Recommendations**: Provide evidence-based treatment suggestions
- **Safety Validation**: Check contraindications and drug interactions

## Use Cases

- Initial health assessment for non-emergency symptoms
- Symptom severity triage (emergency, urgent, routine)
- Medical education and information
- Health concern preliminary evaluation

## Important Disclaimer

This agent provides preliminary health information only and is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical concerns.

## Supported Conditions (25 Total)

**Emergency (9 conditions):**
- Meningitis, Stroke, Heart Attack, Appendicitis, Pulmonary Embolism, Sepsis
- Diabetic Ketoacidosis (DKA), Anaphylaxis, Heat Stroke

**Urgent (7 conditions):**
- Pneumonia, COVID-19, Hypoglycemia, Asthma Exacerbation
- Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT), Kidney Stones, Concussion

**Common/Routine (9 conditions):**
- Migraine, Influenza, Gastroenteritis, Tension Headache, Common Cold
- Urinary Tract Infection (UTI), Dehydration, Food Poisoning, Cellulitis

## Advanced Features

- **Risk-Adjusted Scoring**: Considers age, medical history, and symptom attributes
- **Clinical Scoring Systems**: PERC, Wells, CURB-65, CHA2DS2-VASc, GCS, Centor
- **Lab Test Recommendations**: Blood tests, urinalysis, imaging (X-ray, CT, ultrasound)
- **Treatment Protocol Sequencing**: Step-by-step emergency protocols with timing
- **Epidemiological Intelligence**: Seasonal and geographic disease patterns

## How to Use

Simply describe your symptoms in natural language, including:
- Main symptoms
- Duration
- Severity
- Age and relevant medical history

The agent will analyze your symptoms and provide a comprehensive diagnostic assessment with recommended next steps.
